Why AgriGuard

Rust never sleeps. AgriGuard encapsulates existing rust and seals it away from moisture and oxygen — so you can protect equipment right where it stands, without stripping it back to bare metal.

Coat directly over rustApply straight onto rusted surfaces — no sandblasting, no expensive prep. AgriGuard bonds to and encapsulates the rust.
One-coat protectionA single, durable coat seals out moisture and oxygen — less paint, less labour, more coverage per gallon.
Fast & flexibleQuick-drying and easy to apply by brush, roller or spray — get equipment back to work without the downtime.
