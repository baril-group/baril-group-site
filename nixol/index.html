Why Nixol

Young crops need light — but not too much of it. Nixol lays down a clear, controllable layer of shade that takes the edge off the sun, then washes away without a trace.

Optimal light regulationThe right balance between light transmission and crop protection — bright enough to grow, soft enough to shelter.
Gentle on young cropsSoftens fierce sunlight and heat so seedlings establish calmly, without scorching or stress.
Completely removableWhen the season turns, Nixol washes off cleanly — no peeling, no residue, no surprises on the glass.
Light regulation, not blackout

How it works

Shade that works with the light

Nixol diffuses incoming sunlight into a soft, even glow across the crop. Growers get the climate they want — lower leaf temperatures and gentler peaks — while the canopy still receives the light it needs to grow.

  • Even light diffusion that reduces heat stress and scorch on young plants.
  • Reliable performance on both glass and polycarbonate.
  • Tuned for the growing season, then removed when the light softens.
