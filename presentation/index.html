Kiosk tip. Each slide page loads only its own video, so casting won’t stall. Add ?auto=10 to any slide URL to make it advance to the next slide after 10 seconds (any number of seconds works) — e.g. group/?auto=12. Without ?auto a slide just shows and loops its own video, and your kiosk software controls the rotation. Arrow keys ← → step between slides manually.
