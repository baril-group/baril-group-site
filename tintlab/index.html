RedLike™ tinting system

Every formula, by weight

TintLab is supported by RedLike™ — user-friendly online software to look up colour formulas, with access to thousands of colours from Fleetowner, NCS, RAL and OEM standards worldwide.

  • Look up and match colours from your computer, tablet or phone.
  • Colours are mixed precisely by weight, for tight tolerances.
  • Store customer-specific formulas; receive regular database updates.
