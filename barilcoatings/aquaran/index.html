Why Aquaran

Protection shouldn't cost the air around it. Aquaran delivers durable, industrial-grade coating performance on water — comparable to solvent-based systems, with a fraction of the VOC.

Low VOC emissionsWater-based formulation drastically cuts volatile organic compounds versus solvent coatings.
1K & 2K optionsOne- and two-component systems to match the job — from product finishing to steel protection.
Multi-substrateReliable adhesion on steel, aluminium and galvanised surfaces.
C1–C5 corrosivityEngineered across atmospheric corrosivity classes C1 to C5.
Industrial finishingA clean, consistent finish for OEM and industrial product lines.
Cleaner on the jobLess solvent load means a healthier workplace and a lighter footprint.
