Why Bariline

Not every job needs a two-pack. Bariline keeps steel protection simple — a one-component biobased alkyd that goes straight onto metal, dries fast, and finishes in colour.

One-component simplicityNo mixing, no pot life — a single-pack system that's easy to apply and store.
Biobased alkydAlkyd chemistry built on more renewable raw materials — fossil content designed down.
Direct-to-metalGoes straight onto blasted steel — a primer and finish in one in many applications.
Single-layer optionOne coat of anticorrosive protection, in colour, for steel constructions and containers.
Fast drying & handlingQuick to dry and ready to handle — keeping construction schedules moving.
Colour finishesAnticorrosive performance with a finished colour — from shop primer to topcoat.
