Where it started · Home market

Netherlands

Baril Coatings was founded in 's-Hertogenbosch in 1987. Across the country, our systems protect the steel that the Netherlands runs on — bridges, coastal works and the structures of historic cities.

Baril Coatings 01

Delft · Netherlands

Red arch bridge

A landmark red steel cycle-and-footbridge in the polder near Delft. A three-layer SteelKote system — zinc-rich primer, MIO intermediate and a UV-stable topcoat — keeps the signature red saturated and the steel protected against weather and traffic for decades.

Used coating systems

805 SteelKote EP ZN HS806 SteelKote EP miox808 SteelKote PC HS UV+
Baril Coatings 02

Vlissingen · Netherlands

Michiel de Ruyter boulevard

On the Zeeland coast, steelwork along the Michiel de Ruyter boulevard faces constant salt spray and immersion. A SteelKote MC high-build primer and midcoat under a DualCure finish deliver marine-grade corrosion protection right in the splash zone.

Used coating systems

848 SteelKote MC HB Primer849 SteelKote MC HB Midcoat1712 DualCure Finish

Landmark steel · Stadiums

Romania

In Romania, Baril Coatings protects the structural steel behind some of the country's most visible architecture — national stadiums and infrastructure built to be seen and to last.

Baril Coatings 01

Bucharest · Romania

Stadionul Arcul de Triumf

Bucharest's national rugby stadium. Its structural steel is protected with a SteelKote EP MIO system at 140 µm under a Polydur finish — heavy-duty corrosion protection engineered for a long maintenance interval under sun and weather.

Craiova · Romania

Stadionul Ion Oblemenco

Craiova's football arena, with its sweeping white roof. The same SteelKote EP MIO and Polydur specification protects the exposed steel across the full structure against weather and UV.
