Chapter 01 · DualCure technology

Two cures,
one network

Most industrial coatings cure one way. Our patented DualCure (DCC) cures two ways at once: the chemical 2K reaction of components A and B, plus a second cure driven by moisture from the air. The two networks interlace into one exceptionally dense molecular mesh — bonding tighter and staying more flexible than regular 2K. The result: a multiple of the protection, with far less coating.

Chapter 02 · Thin-layer technology

More metres,

The strength of a coating is not in how thick it is, but in how well it is built. Our thin-layer technology protects more square metres from every litre, at a lower dry-film thickness — less material on the structure, fewer cans on the truck, a smaller footprint per protected m².

Chapter 03 · Renewable chemistry

From barrel
to bio

Every litre of coating is chemistry: binder, solvent, pigment, additives — for a century, crude-oil based. We are converting our industrial coatings to renewable and circular raw materials, from <10% in 2022 to 55% today, on the way to 100% by 2030. Same protection. Lower impact.

Chapter 04 · Corrosion protection

From rust
to resilience

Corrosion is a slow fire: unprotected steel loses tens of microns a year, and structures are replaced long before their time. A Baril protective system seals the steel from oxygen and water — and buys decades. Scroll to apply the coating.
